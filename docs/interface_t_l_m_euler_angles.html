Property Documentation

- (TLMAngle*) pitch
readnonatomicstrong

The pitch of the Myo. This is the vertical angle between your arm exended and a line pointing directly to the horizon. The values will range between -90 to 90 degrees.

Examples:
TLHMViewController.m.
- (TLMAngle*) roll
readnonatomicstrong

The roll of the Myo. This is the angle at which your arm is rotated. Think of a "screwing a lightbulb" motion. The values will range between -180 to 180 degrees.

Examples:
TLHMViewController.m.
- (TLMAngle*) yaw
readnonatomicstrong

The yaw of the Myo. This is the angle your arm is making with respect to the north pole. Imagine your arm as a compass. The values will range between -180 to 180 degrees.

Examples:
TLHMViewController.m.
